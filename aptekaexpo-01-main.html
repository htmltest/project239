Forum-exhibition APTEKA
The international pharmaceutical forum-exhibition APTEKA is a priority exhibition event in Russia, bringing together participants in the pharmaceutical industry — manufacturers and distributors of medicines and medical supplies, representatives of pharmacies and pharmacy chains, medical practitioners, developers of new drugs, representatives of government agencies and health authorities.
APTEKA forum and exhibition provides an opportunity to receive maximum useful information about all important events in the pharmaceutical industry, find new suppliers, place orders for the upcoming season, and acquire additional contacts for further mutually beneficial cooperation.
